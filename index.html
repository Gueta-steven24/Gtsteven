About Me

Multimedia Enthusiast

Hi! I’m Esteven Gueta, a creative and detail-oriented multimedia professional with expertise in graphic design, video editing, photography, and videography. With years of experience in digital media, I specialize in bringing ideas to life through visually compelling content that engages and inspires.

My journey started as a graphic artist and video editor, where I developed strong technical skills using Adobe Premiere Pro, Illustrator, After Effects, Canva, CapCut, and Filmora. I have also worked in printing and production, handling client requests, creating promotional materials, and managing social media pages. Additionally, my experience in sponsor communications, administrative tasks, and financial documentation has sharpened my organizational and business skills.

Beyond design and editing, I create digital products such as busy books, customizable Canva templates, and creative assets to help brands and individuals elevate their content. Whether you need stunning visuals, expertly edited videos, or polished promotional materials, I am committed to delivering high-quality, professional, and creative solutions tailored to your needs.

Let’s work together and bring your ideas to life!
