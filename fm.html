TITLE: Bipolar

2023

CREDITS: Directed by Esteven Gueta • Videography by Christo Geronga and Rea Mae Jocel Cledera • Visual Effects by Cris Ivan Chavez and Josiah Viernes • Edited by Esteven Gueta • Music Background and Sound Direction by Esteven Gueta

A group of university students finds themselves trapped inside their school overnight, only to realize that a deadly game of survival has begun. One by one, their classmates fall victim to a mysterious killer lurking in the shadows. Suspicion grows, fear takes over, and trust shatters as they struggle to uncover the murderer among them. But just when they think they’ve solved the puzzle, the real nightmare begins. The killer they feared is nothing more than a chilling distraction—because the true mastermind has been hiding in plain sight all along. A battle between reality and a fractured mind unfolds, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave them questioning everything. Who will make it out alive? And what happens when the deadliest enemy is the one you never saw coming? Watch now and uncover the terrifying truth! #HorrorThriller #ShortFilm #PsychologicalTwist

TITLE: Bucket List

2023

CREDITS: Directed by Esteven Gueta • Videography by Esteven Gueta • Edited by Esteven Gueta

In a world where dreams often feel out of reach, one student refuses to give up. Juggling school, multiple jobs, and endless responsibilities, she does whatever it takes to support her family while chasing her own ambitions. Through sleepless nights, sacrifices, and moments of doubt, she pushes forward—fueled by determination and love for the people who depend on her. But as the weight of her struggles grows heavier, will she find the strength to keep going, or will life’s challenges break her spirit? This is a story of resilience, hope, and the relentless pursuit of success. Watch her journey unfold. #Inspiration #HardworkPaysOff #NeverGiveUp
